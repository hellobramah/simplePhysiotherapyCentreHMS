About Us

Wineba's first physiotherapy center .

Founded in 2023, Seaside Physiotherapy & Rehabilitation Centre is a premier place for physiotherapy at Winneba and its surroundings in the Central Region of Ghana.

Our initial and most important focus is on You, the Patient. Our aim is to give you consistent and conscientious care, which leads to better outcomes. Our detailed initial consultation helps our physiotherapists to better understand your problems.

Our treatment plans are developed with your goals in mind. This may be a combination of Hands on Techniques as well as carefully thought out Exercise Plans. We will tailor your plan to fit you. If you are in the Winneba area or any of the surrounding townships, and you are looking for relief, look no further.

Seaside Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation Centre is here to help you. Call us at +233 053 44 88 673 to schedule a consultation with one of our Physiotherapists today! Start the path to your recovery at Seaside Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation Centre today!

Our team Seaside Physiotherapy & Rehabilitation Centre has two physiotherapists, one assistant and one intern. Our physiotherapists are compassionate and caring, and eager to help our patients to relieve their pain. They will be happy to work with you and whatever you are struggling with. They will work hard not only to get to the root of your problems and provide quality treatment for the pain you are currently experiencing, but they will also use their abilities to provide you with injury prevention services for the future. Our treatment

We create positive experiences by offering professionalism, proven treatment methods and results that count. Treatment plans are individualized for each patient and are aimed towards the achievements of your goals. When you arrive one of our physiotherapists will design a treatment plan just for you, taking into account your medical symptoms.
